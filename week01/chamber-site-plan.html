Site Plan - Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce. This name represents where the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce seeks to empower the community and surrounding region through community and economic development. We promote business by sharing information about our community and events, as well as providing special services to our members.

Scenarios

  • How can I get in contact with chamber representatives?
  • What are the benefits of becoming a member?
  • What events is the chamber hosting this month to promote the community and local businesses?
